How it works

📖

Quran Lookup

Type quran 2:255 and press SPACE to instantly retrieve Ayatul Kursi.

📚

Hadith Lookup

Type bukhari 1; and press SPACE to insert the first Hadith from Sahih al-Bukhari.

⚡

Works Everywhere

Works natively in Word,Google Doc, Notepad, VS Code, Chrome, WhatsApp, Discord, and everywhere else!

Installation Guide

⚠️ "Windows Protected Your PC" Warning?

Because this app is free and made by an independent developer, Windows Defender SmartScreen might show an "Unknown Publisher" warning when you first open it. This is completely normal!

1

Click on More info in the Windows Defender popup.

2

Click the Run anyway button that appears.
